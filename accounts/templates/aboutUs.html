About Us

Currently there was no sharing platform in University for sharing ongoing or published researches. So, people accross departments did not know about any updates regarding ongoing or completed research. The aim of this project is to provide a common sharing platform for faculties and students of different departments of UoW and keep them updated about the advancements taking place by the members of University.

Members of the faculty have international reputations in biology, chemistry and biochemistry, computer science, earth and environmental science, economics, mathematics and statistics, and physics. More than 650 graduate students and a large number of undergraduates undertake cutting-edge research in cutting-edge facilities, resulting in ground-breaking scientific breakthroughs.

While the University's diverse faculty has unmatched experience in various fields of science, recognised primary research subjects include the environment, biomedical science, advanced materials, and data analytics and visualisation. Professors and students on the Faculty of Science get tens of millions of dollars in research funding each year and publish hundreds of articles in peer-reviewed publications.
